Garlic Chicken & Potatoes

Buttery garlic chicken and potatoes

Ingredients:

  • cut-up chicken 3 to 3 1/2 pounds, should be 9 pieces and breadts halved crosswise
  • 1 pound small potatoes halved (quartered if large)
  • 1 large onion cut into eighths
  • 1 head garlic cloves sepatated and left unpeeled
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary finely minced (you need about 1 tsp.)
  • 1 lemon quartered
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • Kosher slat and ground pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Arrange chicken skin side up, potatoes, onions, garlic, and lemon in a 12Ã—16-inch, glass, roasting pan.
  2. Season chicken and veggies with rosemary, salt and pepper.
  3. Whisk together oil, vinegar and a little salt and pepper and drizzle over the chicken and veggies.
  4. Cook for 50 minutes or until the chicken is browned and cooked through. Enjoy!
  5. Serves 6.
